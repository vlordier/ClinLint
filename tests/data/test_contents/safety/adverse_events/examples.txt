# Example AE reporting text for testing
The subject experienced an adverse event of headache (Grade 1).
The subject had Grade 2 pyrexia.
A serious adverse event (Grade 3 pneumonia) occurred during treatment.
The event was probably related to study drug.
The subject developed Grade 4 neutropenia, definitely related to study drug.
Treatment was discontinued due to Grade 3 hepatotoxicity.
Multiple adverse events were reported: Grade 2 nausea, Grade 1 fatigue.
